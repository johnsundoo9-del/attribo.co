Frequently asked questions

What is Attribo?

Attribo is an AI-search and local-visibility company based in Katy, Texas, serving local and service businesses across Greater Houston. Attribo makes your business the one that AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, Gemini) and Google/Apple Maps read, trust, and recommend — using valid data, answer-first content, and genuine authority.

Is the free check really free?

Yes — completely. We run a live scan of where your business shows up and send you the report, no cost and no obligation. It’s how we show you the problem before you decide anything.

What exactly is AI search visibility?

It’s whether tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews and Gemini — plus Google and Apple Maps — actually name and recommend your business when someone searches for what you do. We measure it and improve it.

How long until I see results?

Honestly: some fixes (like your category and listings) can move things within weeks, but real momentum builds over 2–6 months. Anyone promising overnight results is guessing.

Can you guarantee I’ll be #1 in AI or on the map?

No — and we’ll never claim to. We don’t control the engines, and Google shows each person whatever is closest to them. We guarantee the work, the tracking, and honest monthly reporting that raises your odds.

Where is Attribo based and who does it serve?

Attribo is based in Katy, Texas and serves local and service businesses across Greater Houston — Katy, Cypress, Sugar Land, Richmond, Fulshear, Bellaire, Memorial, and the wider Houston metro.

How do you track where my business shows up?

We run a live grid across your service area — dozens of points on the map — and from each point we check where your business lands when someone searches for what you do, across Google Maps and the AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, Gemini). We repeat it over time so you can see real before-and-after movement. Every number traces back to an actual scan of your business — no guesses and no stock numbers.

Do I need a Google Business Profile to be tracked?

Yes. Your Google Business Profile is what puts you on Google Maps in the first place, so it's what we measure and grow. If you don't have one yet, that's completely fine — setting it up correctly is usually step one, and it's often the single biggest jump in visibility. If you're not on the map at all there's nothing to rank, so we get you on it, then expand your reach from there.
